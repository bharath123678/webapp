About Us

“BHARATH” began its journey in the year 1961, mainly offering logistics and goods transportation services. Over the years, it earned a strong and trustworthy reputation in this field.

Later, in 1991, BHARATH expanded its services to include passenger transport and tour services, helping people travel comfortably and safely.

  • Vision to Serve People

    With a vision to serve people better and provide medical support, BHARATH started ambulance services in the year 2013. It began with basic ambulance services for those needing quick transportation during health emergencies.

  • Advanced Life Support Services

    As the need grew, BHARATH also introduced D-level ICU ambulance services, which are specially equipped to give life support to patients while they are being taken to the hospital. These services have been availed across the country.

  • 64 Years of Service

    Today, BHARATH proudly looks back on 64 years of service in different areas of transportation, always focused on helping people and saving lives.

Basic Life Support (BLS) Ambulance

This ambulance is for non-emergency or non-critical cases. It is suitable for patients who do not require advanced medical attention during transport, such as:

  • Patients going for routine checkups or follow-ups
  • Elderly or disabled persons needing transport
  • Stable patients being discharged or moved to another facility. It is usually staffed with trained drivers and emergency medical technicians (EMTs), and contains essential equipment like oxygen, stretcher, and first aid supplies.

Advanced Life Support (ALS) Ambulance

An ALS ambulance is equipped to handle serious medical emergencies. It includes advanced equipment like:

  • Ventilators
  • Cardiac monitors
  • Defibrillators
  • IV infusion pumps

It is staffed with critical care nurses who can provide medication, advanced airway management, and cardiac support in the ambulance.

ICU on Wheels

This is a Mobile Intensive Care Unit (M-ICU), created for critically ill or unstable patients who need constant monitoring and life-saving care during transportation.

Key features include:

  • ICU-grade equipment (ventilators, defibrillators, syringe pumps)
  • Onboard power backup and oxygen supply
  • Staffed with an intensive care doctor and nurse

Ideal for:

  • Inter-hospital transfers of ICU patients
  • Long-distance medical transfers

Neonatal Ambulance

Specially designed for newborns and premature babies who require urgent medical attention.

It is equipped with:

  • Infant incubators
  • Oxygen and suction units
  • Monitors suited for babies

A neonatal nurse usually accompany the infant. This service ensures safe and temperature-controlled transport for delicate newborns to specialized hospitals.

Dead Body Transport / Mortuary Van

This service is used for the respectful and hygienic transfer of deceased persons from one place to another.

It is equipped with:

  • Body freezer or cooling system
  • Proper securing setup for the body
  • Transport from hospital to home or cremation ground
  • Long-distance transfers to native places
  • Repatriation of remains

Event Medical Support

BHARATH provides medical assistance for public or private events where large gatherings are expected.

Events include:

  • Marathons
  • Religious festivals
  • Political rallies
  • Corporate events or concerts

Trained staff, ambulances, and first aid stations are set up at event locations to handle any medical emergency on the spot.

Inter-Hospital Transfers

This service is used to move patients from one hospital to another, either for:

  • Accessing better treatment or specialized care
  • Shifting from one hospitals to another

Based on the patient’s condition, BLS, ALS, or ICU ambulances can be used. The focus is on a smooth and safe transfer while maintaining medical care throughout the journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore our FAQs to find quick answers to common questions about our ambulance services, response times, and how we ensure your safety and care during emergencies.

What types of ambulance services do you offer?

We provide Basic Life Support (BLS), Advanced Life Support (ALS), and Patient Transport services to cater to various medical emergencies and non-emergency needs.

How quickly can an ambulance arrive in an emergency?s

Our ambulances are strategically located to ensure rapid response times, typically arriving within minutes based on your location and traffic conditions.

Are your ambulances equipped for all medical emergencies?

Yes, our ambulances come fully equipped with advanced medical equipment and staffed by trained healthcare professionals ready to handle a wide range of emergencies.

Can I book a non-emergency ambulance for scheduled transport?

Absolutely, we offer scheduled non-emergency ambulance transportation for medical appointments, hospital transfers, and other healthcare needs.

Is your ambulance service available 24/7?

Yes, we operate round-the-clock to provide emergency and non-emergency transport any time you need us.

What should I do while waiting for the ambulance to arrive?

Stay calm, ensure the patient is comfortable, and if appropriate, follow any first aid instructions provided by our team or emergency dispatchers.

Do you accept insurance for ambulance services?

We work with most major insurance providers. Please check with our support team or your insurer to understand coverage details.
